# 🇮🇩 03 — Indonesia Business Trends

The gold-mine folder. Mine demand, struggles, bottlenecks, then match each with a sellable solution.

## What to capture per signal

- **The pain** (verbatim from real user)
- **Source** (URL + date + platform)
- **Volume** (how many people expressing it)
- **Existing solutions** (and why they fail)
- **Your wedge** (how you'd solve it cheaper/faster/better)
- **Price people would pay** (evidence, not guess)

## Sub-folders

- `demand-mining/` — raw pain points scraped from forums, marketplace, social
- `bottlenecks/` — regulatory, logistics, payment, talent gaps
- `struggles/` — UMKM, freelancer, employee, student pains
- `news-feed/` — daily Indonesian business/tech news clippings
- `proposals/` — sales pitches ready to send
- `competitors/` — who already plays here, pricing, weaknesses
- `playbooks/` — how to approach, sell, deliver

## Sources to mine systematically

- [ ] Kompas, Detik, Tempo, CNN ID headlines
- [ ] Kontan, Bisnis.com, Investor ID for market/business
- [ ] Twitter ID trends (`#umkm`, `#startup`, `#freelancer`, `#loker`)
- [ ] LinkedIn ID job posts (what skills are scarce?)
- [ ] Telegram groups: lowoker, info-loker, umkm-indonesia
- [ ] Marketplace: Tokopedia best-seller categories, Shopee trending
- [ ] Jobstreet / Glints top job titles
- [ ] YouTube ID business channels (new uploads/day)

## High-signal Indonesian struggles (seed list)

- UMKM: tidak bisa dapat modal, ribet invoice, gak ngerti marketing digital
- Freelancer: cari client susah, payment luar negeri mahal, revisi tanpa batas
- Karyawan: side hustle tapi waktu habis, skill obsolete, gaji stuck
- Student: magang gratis, skill tidak relevan, fresh grad unemployed
- Parents: biaya sekolah naik, les mahal, susu formula mahal
