# 💎 07 — Gaps & Opportunities

The synthesis layer. Everything from folders 01-06 feeds here. The job: **never stop finding the next demand that nobody is serving well.**

## How to use this folder

1. **Daily:** drop raw observations into `inbox/` as you encounter them
2. **Weekly:** promote the strongest signals into `opportunities/` with a one-pager
3. **Monthly:** archive stale ones into `graveyard/`, refresh `playbook.md`
4. **Quarterly:** write `thesis.md` — your evolving view of where the money is

## Sub-folders

- `inbox/` — raw signals, unfiltered (one file per observation)
- `opportunities/` — vetted plays with: pain, evidence, wedge, price, build cost, time-to-revenue
- `graveyard/` — things you explored and killed (record why)
- `playbook.md` — your operating principles (evolving)
- `thesis.md` — long-form quarterly essay
- `templates/opportunity-onepager.md` — copy this for each new play

## Cross-topic synthesis prompts

Run these every Friday:

1. "What recurring pain shows up in BOTH `01-` (social signals) AND `03-` (ID business)?"
2. "Which gap in `06-` (regional prices) is being mispriced by a national player?"
3. "What can `04-` (freelancer agent) deliver that today's `02-` (trading bot) market demands?"
4. "What scraper in `01-` would turn into a product in `03-`?"
