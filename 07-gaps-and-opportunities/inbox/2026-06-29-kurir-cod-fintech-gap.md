# Kurir COD Instant Cash-Out Fintech

**Date:** 2026-06-29
**Discovered during:** demand-mining kurir logistik pain point research

Millions of courier riders (J&T, JNE, SiCepat, Shopee Express) have their COD money held for 7-14 days. They front fuel and operational costs while platforms sit on their cash. A fintech that instant-cashes COD payments (like "pay later" in reverse) would capture massive daily transaction volume. Add micro-insurance for package loss/damage and vehicle financing based on delivery history instead of credit score. Estimated addressable market: 2M+ active courier riders in Indonesia.
