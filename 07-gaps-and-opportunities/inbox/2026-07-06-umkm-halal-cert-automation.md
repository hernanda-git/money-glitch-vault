# [2026-07-06] UMKM Halal Certification Automation Platform

Pain: 40-60% of micro-UMKM lack NPWP (prerequisite for NIB and halal certification). BPJPH sanctions start October 2026, only 3 months away. P3H (halal certification companions) are scarce and unevenly distributed. Current self-declare system is too complex for most micro-UMKM.

Pitch: A "HalalReady" SaaS platform that walks UMKM through the entire certification chain (NPWP -> NIB -> SEHATI halal cert) with an interactive wizard, integrates with OSS for document processing, provides a marketplace for P3H companions, and sends automated deadline notifications. Could also serve as a bridge between the existing P3H matching platform (07-gaps) and the UMKM digitalization gap (03).
