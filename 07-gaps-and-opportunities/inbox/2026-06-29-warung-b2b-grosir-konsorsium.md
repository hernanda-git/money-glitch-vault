# Warung B2B Grosir Konsorsium

**Date:** 2026-06-29
**Discovered during:** demand-mining warung tradisional vs minimarket research

3.5 million traditional warungs in Indonesia are being crushed by Alfamart/Indomaret (43,000+ stores combined) because they buy at retail prices while minimarkets buy wholesale. A B2B grosir platform that aggregates 100-500 warungs per area to buy collectively from tier-1 distributors could restore competitive pricing. Warungs get wholesale prices, platform takes 3-5% margin (vs distributor 15-25% markup). Cross-sell: micro-loans based on sales data, local marketing tools, QRIS payment integration.
