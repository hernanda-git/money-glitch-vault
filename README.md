## 🔁 Operating Rhythm

- **Daily:** cron jobs push fresh data into `05-` and `06-`; you eyeball signals in `01-` and `03-`
- **Weekly:** synthesize `07-gaps-and-opportunities/weekly-gap-report.md` — what new demand surfaced, what to build/sell
- **Monthly:** archive dead leads, upgrade working bots, add new scrapers

## 🧰 Conventions

- One markdown per insight/source. Filename = slug of the claim
- Always note **source URL, capture date, signal strength (1-5)**
- Scripts live next to the data they produce: `topic/scripts/`
- Anything that becomes a real product graduates to its own repo

## ⚠️ Ethics

Scraping with real cookies/tokens = your account, your risk. Never resell scraped personal data. Trading bots = not financial advice. Everything here is **research & tooling**, not a turnkey money printer.
